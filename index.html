About Me

Hi there! I'm a student who happens to enjoy designing products and experience based on the needs and convenience of user. I begun my tech journey at age 12 with web development when I decided to learn to add autoplaying video as website background— turns out, doing this and playing a welcome voice on page-load taught me a lot about HTML & CSS! Fast-forward to today, I’m a student of Higher Secondary School and have had the privilege of working freelance and part-time with clients/startups/businesses all around the globe, while also being equally invested & interested in building products. I understand that great work isn’t done in isolation but is a dance between the creator and the world. I’m a business-minded driven designer who thoroughly enjoys engaging in all steps of the product life-cycle. My focus is on creating/designing products that look beautiful and function intuitively for the user.
