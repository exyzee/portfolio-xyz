About Me

From teaching myself to code at the age of 12 to working with leading companies before graduating high school, my journey has been anything but ordinary. Starting as a self-taught developer, I quickly discovered a passion for creating meaningful, user-centered digital experiences, blending technical expertise with a sharp eye for design. By the time I transitioned into Product Design in 2020, my background as a developer gave me a unique edge—an ability to bridge the gap between scalable technical solutions and intuitive user experiences. Since then, I’ve worked across diverse industries, including Web3, tackling challenges in DeFi, wallets, social platforms, consumer products, and DAOs. My focus remains on solving complex UX problems and crafting interfaces that feel effortless for users.

Collaboration and feedback are at the heart of my approach. I believe that exceptional work is born from shared ideas and iterative refinement, and I actively seek feedback to elevate both functionality and aesthetics in everything I build.

Beyond design and development, I’ve had the privilege of speaking at global Web3 conferences, sharing my insights on UX and product strategy with international audiences.

With a career rooted in curiosity and driven by a love for innovation, I’m always pushing boundaries to create products that shape the future. Whether solving a tough UX challenge or exploring emerging technologies, I’m excited about the possibilities ahead and the impact I can make..
